Frequently Asked Questions About Immunizations & Vaccinations

What immunizations and vaccinations do you offer at All In One Care Solutions Medical Center in Miami Lakes?
At All In One Care Solutions Medical Center, we offer a comprehensive range of immunizations and vaccinations for both adults and children. This includes routine vaccines such as flu shots, Tdap, MMR, hepatitis A and B, HPV, and varicella, as well as travel vaccines and COVID-19 vaccinations. If you have specific vaccine requirements or are preparing for school, work, or travel, please call ahead so we can ensure availability.
How much do vaccinations cost, and do you accept insurance at your Miami Lakes location?
The cost of vaccinations can vary depending on the type of vaccine. For example, flu shots typically range from $30 to $50 without insurance, while other vaccines like shingles or travel immunizations may be higher. We accept most major insurance plans, which often cover routine immunizations at little or no out-of-pocket cost. If you’re uninsured or underinsured, we offer self-pay options and can provide a price estimate before your visit. Please bring your insurance card and a photo ID when you come in.
How soon can I get an appointment for vaccinations, and do I need to schedule in advance?
We offer same-day and next-day appointments for most immunizations at our Miami Lakes office. Walk-ins are welcome for routine vaccines, but we recommend scheduling ahead if you need a specific vaccine or require multiple shots, especially during busy seasons like back-to-school or flu season. You can book an appointment by phone or through our website.
Are your providers licensed and qualified to administer vaccines?
Absolutely. All of our healthcare providers are fully licensed in the state of Florida and have extensive experience administering vaccines. Our medical staff stays up to date with CDC guidelines and follows all recommended protocols to ensure safe and effective vaccination for every patient.
What is your process for immunizations, and do you guarantee vaccine storage and handling safety?
When you arrive at our Miami Lakes medical center, you'll complete a brief health screening to determine which vaccines are appropriate for you. Our staff will review your vaccination history and answer any questions you may have. All vaccines are stored and handled according to strict CDC standards, and we guarantee proper refrigeration and expiration monitoring to ensure safety and efficacy. After your shot, we monitor you for any immediate reactions and provide you with a record of your immunizations for your records or school/work requirements.
